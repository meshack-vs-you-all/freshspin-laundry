## Information We Collect

When you use our services or contact us, we may collect:

- **Name** — to identify your laundry order
- **Phone number** — for WhatsApp communication and order updates
- **Location** — for pickup and delivery within Kahawa Wendani
- **Laundry preferences** — detergent, fabric care, and folding instructions

## How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

- Process and fulfill your laundry orders
- Communicate order status and delivery updates via WhatsApp
- Notify you of promotions and loyalty rewards (only if you opt in)
- Improve our services based on customer feedback

## Data Sharing

{{< alert icon="lock" >}}
**We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.** Your data stays with FreshSpin Laundry and is used exclusively for service delivery.
{{< /alert >}}

## Data Storage & Security

- Your contact information is stored securely on our internal systems
- We do not store payment card information — all payments are handled in person or via M-Pesa
- We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access** the personal data we hold about you
- **Request correction** of inaccurate information
- **Request deletion** of your data at any time
